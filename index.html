Our Journey.

When Curious Men Films set out to tell the stories of India's founders, they didn't know the journey would take them across 21 cities and into the lives of over 100 entrepreneurs.

2016

Ritesh and Aniruddha crossed paths in Mumbai during their internship at Sikhya Entertainment. A shared love for storytelling brought them together.

2017

They decided to stop waiting and start making. Curious Men Films was founded with one goal — tell stories that matter.

2018

From music videos and short films to ads and corporate films — they worked with brands like Air India, Amul, Kingfisher, Practo & Aditya Birla.

2022

While shooting TechSparks in Bangalore, they met Prateek Sethi — and discovered Shifu Ventures and Shifu Foundation.

2024

They hit the road and traveled across 21 cities, filming founders from the most unexpected corners of India.

2026

The journey became a film. Against All Odds was completed in 2026 — and with it, a new niche was born.
